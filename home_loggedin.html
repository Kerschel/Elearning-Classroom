Virtual Knowledge

E-learning Cloud platform customizable for you

Manage students online all over the world

Students all over the world can register for your classes and can recieve content regardless of how far away they are. Lectures can be given, assignments and exams can be graded and submitted without without being in the same geographical location.

Focus on teaching, not managing students and grading

Virtual knowledge removes the challenge of having to collect and grade assignment or exams. To students can upload submissions to the web application and let virtual knowledge do the rest. Lecturers have the choice of letting virtual knowledge grade submissions and deliver results to students or grading submissions personally.

Track students progress

Get analytics on students grades through virtual knowledge to see how students are progressing in certain topics and if more attention is required.
